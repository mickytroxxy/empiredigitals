Valid

Valid is a new fashion brand that aims to revolutionize the fashion industry by using QR codes and a mobile app to verify the authenticity of its products and protect the Valid brand and other participating brands from counterfeiting. Our mobile app allows customers to scan the QR code on their clothing and instantly access information such as the product's purchase price, purchase date, and the buyer's name and face. The app also allows customers to verify the brand's name and track the location of their clothing in case it gets lost.

MrDocs

An app that uses biometric signatures to sign documents electronically. It allows users to simply upload a document, such as an ID, contract, NDA, or any other document that requires a signature, and then press the "sign" button. The front camera will then appear, allowing the user to take a selfie, which is embedded into a QR code that is attached to the document. To verify who signed the document, simply scan the QR code using our app. All signees' faces will appear, making it easy to verify the identity of the person who signed the document. We also uses facial recognition technology to ensure only the rightful person can sign the document
